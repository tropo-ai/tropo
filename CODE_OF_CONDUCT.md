# Code of Conduct

Tropo follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and help newcomers. Harassment, discrimination, or abuse of any kind is not tolerated, in issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other community space.

To report a concern, email hello@tropo-ai.com. Reports are handled discreetly.
