Notes are the lightest thing in the system. An insight, a question, an observation, a half-formed idea — anything worth remembering but not worth a full task.

## What to do

1. Wait for it. While you're working on the other tasks — or just thinking about your project — something will occur to you. An idea. A concern. A "we should think about..." moment.
2. When it happens, tell your agent: "Capture that as a note."
3. Your agent creates a note in the Vault. Title, a line of context, done.

## Why this matters

Ideas disappear. The ones that matter most often arrive in the middle of something else. Notes are designed to be fast enough that capturing the thought doesn't break your flow. You can organize them later — or not. The point is the idea doesn't get lost.

## When you're done

You've captured at least one real thought as a note. Mark this task done.
