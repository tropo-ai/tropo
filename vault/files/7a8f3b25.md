## Purpose

Publishes the finalized draft to its primary surface (typically Substack for essay-shaped content). Records the published URL on the activation-root frontmatter.

## Structure

Leaf step. No children.

## Nodes

No child nodes. Leaf WorkflowNode.

## Flow Rules

Completes when: the draft is live on the primary surface and the activation root's `substack_url:` (or equivalent surface-pointer field) is populated with the published URL.

Advances to: syndicate (f4d2e617). Typically the executor waits T+3-5 days before advancing to give the primary publication a window before syndication.

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

The author or agent driving the run publishes the finalized draft to Substack (or the configured primary surface). The published URL is captured and written to the activation-root frontmatter as `substack_url:` (or surface-appropriate field). Step closes. The executor records the publish timestamp; the T+3-5 day window before syndicate is convention rather than schema-enforced.

## Known Enforcement Gaps

Surface-specific URL field naming is convention at v1.0.0. Multi-surface release where the primary surface varies (some pieces are Substack-primary, others LinkedIn-primary, others Tropo.ai-primary) is supported by convention; formalization in future per-surface capsules.
