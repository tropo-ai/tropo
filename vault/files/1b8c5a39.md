## Purpose

Captures reader engagement on the published surfaces during the active reply window. Produces an engagement record (`type: note`, free-form per [design brief d3a5f1b9](d3a5f1b9.md) Q3) as a member of the activation root. Engagement record captures whatever the author finds worth recording — top comments, threads that led to follow-on work, sentiment, conversion events.

## Structure

Leaf step. No children.

## Nodes

No child nodes. Leaf WorkflowNode.

## Flow Rules

Completes when: ~24-48 hours have passed after each surface publish AND an engagement record is committed as a member of the activation root.

Advances to: repost-amplify (6e3d9c47).

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

After each surface publish (step-6 + step-7), the author actively engages reader comments for ~24-48 hours — replying to substantive comments, threading conversations, capturing what's notable. When the active engagement window closes, the author writes an engagement record as a `type: note` member of the activation root. Free-form: no required fields. Captures whatever was worth noting from this piece's reader response.

## Known Enforcement Gaps

Engagement-record format is `type: note` (free-form) per design brief Q3. If patterns repeat across many pieces — every author wanting to record the same fields — graduate to a dedicated `engagement-record.capsule` in a future cycle. Earn-the-abstraction discipline.

Duration semantics (24-48 hour window) are convention rather than schema-enforced.
