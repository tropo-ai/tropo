You're looking at a task right now. This is what tasks look like in Tropo — a governed file with a title, a description, a status, and a body that explains what needs to be done.

## What to do

1. Think of something you actually need to do. Not a test — something real. A report to write, a decision to make, a conversation to have.
2. Tell your agent: "Create a task for me to [whatever it is]."
3. Your agent will create a new file in the Vault with a unique ID, add it to the index, and confirm.

## Why this matters

This is the moment your vault stops being a demo and starts being yours. The task you just created lives alongside these seed tasks in the same system, governed by the same rules. There's no difference between "getting started" content and "real" content. It's all governed work.

## When you're done

You have a real task in your vault that matters to you. Mark this one done.
