## Purpose

Plan and execute event staffing: travel roster; booth-shift schedule; customer meeting bookings; session-attendance assignments. Owned by `staffing-coordinator-agent`.

## Structure

```
staffing-workstream
├── Travel roster confirmed (who's going)
├── Booth shifts assigned
├── Customer meetings scheduled
├── Session-attendance assignments (who covers which talks)
└── Day-of dispatch (final logistics; backup roster)
```

## Nodes

Leaf workstream; tasks generated by `staffing-coordinator-agent`.

## Flow Rules

Linear; travel roster confirmed first (dependent on Finance budget allocation in master plan); shifts/meetings flow from confirmed roster.

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

`staffing-coordinator-agent` generates tasks against project plan: roster confirmation; shift assignments via team scheduling; customer meeting outreach + booking; per-attendee session-coverage assignments. Cross-team coordination with Hospitality (who attends reception) + Promotion (executive-led press meetings).

## Known Enforcement Gaps

None at v1.35.0 minimal demonstration shape.
