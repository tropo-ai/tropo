# New Hire First-Week Onboarding

*Reference Playbook | Demonstrates: Groups model, milestone DAG, parallel execution, pauses, boundary calls, composition, conditional paths, run folder, verification receipts.*
*This is the canonical example that proves Playbook Spec v2.1 is executable at L1.*

---

## Intent

Successfully integrate a new employee into the organization during their first week. By the end of Day 5, the employee has physical and systems access, has completed required compliance training, has met their team and immediate stakeholders, has a reviewed 30/60/90 plan, and has completed their first real work assignment. HR, IT, and the hiring manager have all attested to completion of their respective responsibilities.

This process spans 5 days, involves 3 organizational roles plus the new employee, crosses 3 external systems, and includes 3 human attestation gates. No single agent or person completes this alone — the Playbook coordinates everyone.

---

## Suggestions

- Begin pre-boarding at least 5 business days before the start date. Failures in Group 1 cascade into Day 1.
- The 30/60/90 plan (Group 6) is the hiring manager's most important contribution. Do not let it be an afterthought.
- Benefits enrollment has a hard 30-day deadline from the start date. Open it on Day 1, close it before Day 30.
- If compliance training is not completed by Day 14, flag immediately — most jurisdictions require it within the first two weeks.
- Remote vs. in-office determines Groups 2 and 4 branch paths. Confirm location before starting Group 1.
- Before each step that crosses a boundary, write `event: step_started` to `run.jsonl` BEFORE the call fires. If the executor crashes mid-step, the next executor can distinguish "never started" from "started but not completed."
- Every boundary call to an external system must include an idempotency key: `run:{run_uid}:step:{step_id}`. Pass it to the external system so retries do not duplicate records, emails, or enrollments.
- `thread.md` accumulates context across sessions. When it exceeds ~8,000 tokens, summarize: condense completed group context to 2-3 sentences per group, keep current state and open items in full. The summary replaces the prior content. Full history is in `run.jsonl`.

---

## Rules

- The new employee's system accounts must be provisioned BEFORE their start date. Late provisioning on Day 1 blocks Groups 3, 4, and 5.
- No employee may begin work with access to production systems before completing the security and data handling modules in compliance training.
- The 30/60/90 plan must be reviewed and signed by the hiring manager within the first 5 business days.
- Benefits enrollment must be opened on Day 1 and completed within 30 days of the start date. Missing this window cannot be corrected without a qualifying life event.
- All attestation records must be stored in the HRIS system and referenced in the run's `run.jsonl`. Verbal attestations are not sufficient.

### Run Artifacts
This playbook executes inside a run folder. Before Group 1 begins the executor must confirm these exist (reference: `.tropo/capsules/playbook-run.capsule.md` — supplementary, not required to proceed):
- **Run folder:** `playbook-runs/new-hire-onboarding-{run_uid}-{YYYY-MM-DD}/`
- **run_uid:** 8-character hex UID generated at run creation (`openssl rand -hex 4`)
- **run.jsonl:** append-only event log in the run folder. Schema: `{"event": "<type>", "timestamp": "YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM", "step": "<step_id or null>", "detail": "<string>"}`
- **thread.md:** session context accumulator in the run folder. Append across sessions. Groom per Suggestions above.
- **context.md:** filled at invocation with: new hire name, start date, location (remote/in-office), hiring manager, HR manager contact. This is the Tier 4 binding.
- **artifacts/:** empty directory in the run folder, ready for produced files

## Groups

*Execution model: Groups 1-3 are sequential. Groups 2 and 3 are parallel once Group 1 is complete. Groups 4, 5, 6 form a DAG. Group 7 runs parallel with a time gate.*

```
Group 1 — Pre-boarding
 ↓ [Pre-boarding Complete]
Group 2 — Physical Access (parallel) Group 3 — HR Documentation (parallel)
 ↓ [Physical Access Ready] ↓ [HR Documentation Complete]
Group 4 — Systems Setup
(depends on: Physical Access Ready)
 ↓ [Systems Access Complete]
Group 5 — Orientation
(depends on: Systems Access Complete + HR Documentation Complete)
 ↓ [Week 1 Orientation Complete]
Group 6 — Work Integration
(depends on: Week 1 Orientation Complete)
 ↓ [Week 1 Integration Complete]

Group 7 — Benefits Enrollment (parallel throughout, time-gated: 30 days)
 ↓ [Benefits Enrollment Complete]
```

### Group 1 — Pre-boarding
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Parallel:** no
**Depends on:** none (start here)
**Milestone:** Pre-boarding Complete
**Milestone timeout:** 3 days before start date — if Pre-boarding Complete has not fired, escalate to HR Director immediately

All of Group 1 must complete before the employee's first day. Failures here cascade.

#### Step 1.1 — Initiate Background Check
**Executor:** agent
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** 10 business days before start date
**Produces:** Background check initiated; case number logged to `run.jsonl`
**Boundary call:**
 type: service
 target: background-check-provider
 idempotency_key: "run:{run_uid}:step:1.1"
 validation: "Case number received and status is 'in progress'"
 max_retries: 2

Write `event: step_started, step: "1.1"` to `run.jsonl` before initiating the call.

Initiate the background check through the provider. Log the case number.

#### Step 1.2 — Order Equipment
**Executor:** human (IT Coordinator)
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** 7 business days before start date
**Produces:** Equipment order confirmation; delivery tracking number logged
**Boundary call:**
 type: human
 target: IT Coordinator
 channel: [binding: coordination]
 validation: "Equipment ordered with confirmed delivery date before start date"
 max_retries: 1
 escalation: HR Manager if no confirmation within 48h
**Pause after:** human-attestation

If remote employee: order to home address, add external monitor and webcam to the standard kit.
If in-office employee: order to office address.

#### Step 1.3 — Provision System Accounts
**Executor:** human (IT Coordinator)
**Owner:** IT Coordinator
**Deadline:** 2 business days before start date
**Produces:** Email, Slack, HRIS account confirmed active; logged to `run.jsonl`
**Depends on:** Step 1.2 confirmed (we know where they're working)
**Boundary call:**
 type: service
 target: access-management
 validation: "Email, Slack, and HRIS accounts active and accessible"

Provision email, Slack, HRIS access, and VPN credentials. Do NOT provision production system access until Step 5.3 compliance training is complete.

#### Step 1.4 — Clear Background Check
**Executor:** agent (monitoring)
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** 3 business days before start date
**Produces:** Background check status logged as 'cleared'
**Boundary call:**
 type: service
 target: background-check-provider
 state: waiting
 validation: "Status is 'cleared'"
 max_retries: 0
 escalation: HR Director immediately if any flag
**Pause after:** external-system

If not cleared by deadline: pause all remaining steps, escalate to HR Director immediately. Do not proceed to Day 1 without clearance.

#### Step 1.5 — Send Day 1 Agenda
**Executor:** agent
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** 1 business day before start date
**Produces:** Welcome email sent; logged

Send the welcome email using the [New Employee Welcome Email] template. Include: Day 1 schedule, office location or video link, who to ask for, what to bring.

### Group 2 — Physical Access
**Owner:** Facilities
**Parallel:** yes (runs concurrently with Group 3 after Group 1 completes)
**Depends on:** Pre-boarding Complete
**Milestone:** Physical Access Ready
**Milestone timeout:** 8:00 AM on start date — if not fired, IT issues a loaner laptop and facilities escalates badge issuance immediately

*If remote employee: Steps 2.1 and 2.2 are replaced by Step 2R — confirm home equipment delivery and schedule virtual Day 1 call with IT.*

#### Step 2.1 — Issue Badge
**Executor:** human (Facilities)
**Owner:** Facilities
**Deadline:** Before 9:00 AM on start date
**Produces:** Badge issued; access management record created
**Boundary call:**
 type: human
 target: Facilities
 channel: [binding: coordination]
 validation: "Badge issued and building access active"
 max_retries: 0
 hard_deadline: Start date 8:00 AM
**Pause after:** human-attestation

Facilities issues the badge and activates building access. Posts confirmation to coordination channel before 9:00 AM on start date.

#### Step 2.2 — Laptop Delivery Confirmed
**Executor:** agent (monitoring)
**Owner:** IT Coordinator
**Deadline:** Before start date
**Boundary call:**
 type: service
 target: shipping-provider
 validation: "Delivery confirmed at office address"
 max_retries: 0
 hard_deadline: Start date 8:00 AM
**Pause after:** external-system

If not delivered: escalate immediately, issue loaner laptop for Day 1.

---

### Group 3 — HR Documentation
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Parallel:** yes (runs concurrently with Group 2)
**Depends on:** Pre-boarding Complete
**Milestone:** HR Documentation Complete
**Milestone timeout:** End of Day 1 — if not fired, HR Manager escalates directly to employee and hiring manager

#### Step 3.1 — Collect Employment Documentation
**Executor:** human (New Employee) + agent (coordination)
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** By end of Day 1
**Produces:** Signed NDA, IP assignment, tax forms — stored in HRIS
**Boundary call:**
 type: human
 target: New Employee
 channel: HRIS system
 validation: "NDA, IP assignment, I-9, and tax forms all completed and in HRIS"
 max_retries: 1
 retry_cadence: same-day reminder at 3:00 PM
 escalation: HR Manager if incomplete by end of Day 1
**Pause after:** external-system

Agent coordinates with the new employee to complete all employment documentation in the HRIS system. All documents must be signed and stored — not just acknowledged.

#### Step 3.2 — Open Benefits Enrollment
**Executor:** agent
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** Day 1
**Produces:** Benefits enrollment opened in HRIS; employee notified of 30-day deadline

Open benefits enrollment in the HRIS system. Send the new employee a clear note: enrollment window closes 30 days from today, not 30 days from when they get around to it. Log enrollment window open date to `run.jsonl`. This starts the Group 7 clock.

---

### Group 4 — Systems Setup
**Owner:** IT Coordinator
**Parallel:** no
**Depends on:** Physical Access Ready
**Milestone:** Systems Access Complete
**Milestone timeout:** 2:00 PM Day 1 — if not fired, IT Coordinator escalates to IT Manager

*This group configures the actual laptop/workstation. Requires physical access to be confirmed first.*

#### Step 4.1 — Configure Laptop
**Executor:** human (IT Coordinator) + new employee
**Owner:** IT Coordinator
**Deadline:** By noon on Day 1
**Produces:** Laptop configured, encrypted, VPN active, password manager set up

IT Coordinator walks the new employee through laptop setup: FileVault/BitLocker encryption, VPN configuration, password manager setup, standard application installation.

If remote: IT sends a self-guided setup doc; schedules a 30-minute video call to verify completion.

#### Step 4.2 — Verify System Access
**Executor:** agent
**Owner:** IT Coordinator
**Deadline:** By 2:00 PM on Day 1
**Produces:** Access verification logged to `run.jsonl`
**Boundary call:**
 type: human
 target: New Employee
 validation: "Employee can log in to email, Slack, HRIS, and VPN without errors"
 max_retries: 1

Agent sends a checklist to the new employee: can you log in to each system? Employee confirms or flags issues. IT resolves any issues same day.

---

### Group 5 — Orientation
**Owner:** HR Manager + Hiring Manager
**Parallel:** no
**Depends on:** Systems Access Complete, HR Documentation Complete
**Milestone:** Week 1 Orientation Complete
**Milestone timeout:** End of Day 5 — if not fired, HR Director notified

#### Step 5.1 — Company and Product Orientation
**Executor:** human (Hiring Manager)
**Owner:** Hiring Manager
**Deadline:** By end of Day 2
**Produces:** Orientation meeting completed; employee confirms understanding of company mission and product

Hiring manager leads a 90-minute session: company history, product overview, business model, the team's role in the larger mission. This is context, not process — the employee should understand *why* the work matters.

#### Step 5.2 — Meet the Team
**Executor:** human (HR Manager + Hiring Manager)
**Owner:** Hiring Manager
**Deadline:** By end of Day 2
**Produces:** Team introductions complete; employee has met each team member 1:1

Schedule individual 30-minute 1:1s between the new employee and each team member. Brief each team member in advance: this is for relationship-building, not role explanation.

#### Step 5.3 — Complete Compliance Training
**Executor:** human (New Employee) via LMS
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** By end of Day 5 (hard); Day 14 regulatory limit
**Produces:** All required modules completed and attested in LMS; completion certificate logged to HRIS
**Boundary call:**
 type: service
 target: lms
 validation: "All required modules for this role completed with passing scores"
 max_retries: 0
 hard_deadline: Day 14 (regulatory requirement)
**Pause after:** external-system

Invoke the [Compliance Training] sub-playbook. Required modules: security awareness, data handling, harassment prevention, and any role-specific modules. Production system access is unlocked only after this step completes.

If remote: all modules are available online. No exceptions.

#### Step 5.4 — Review and Attest Company Handbook
**Executor:** human (New Employee)
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** By end of Day 3
**Produces:** Signed attestation in HRIS
**Boundary call:**
 type: human
 target: New Employee
 channel: HRIS system
 validation: "Handbook attestation signed and dated in HRIS"
 max_retries: 1
 escalation: HR Manager day 4 if unsigned
**Pause after:** human-attestation

---

### Group 6 — Work Integration
**Owner:** Hiring Manager
**Parallel:** no
**Depends on:** Week 1 Orientation Complete
**Milestone:** Week 1 Integration Complete
**Milestone timeout:** End of Day 5 — if not fired, HR Director notified

#### Step 6.1 — Create and Review 30/60/90 Plan
**Executor:** human (Hiring Manager) + new employee
**Owner:** Hiring Manager
**Deadline:** By end of Day 5
**Produces:** Signed 30/60/90 plan stored in HRIS; copy in `artifacts/30-60-90-plan.md`
**Boundary call:**
 type: human
 target: Hiring Manager
 channel: [binding: coordination]
 validation: "30/60/90 plan reviewed, discussed with employee, and signed by both parties"
 max_retries: 0
 hard_deadline: Day 5 end of business
**Pause after:** human-attestation

Hiring manager drafts the 30/60/90 plan using the template in Resources. Schedules a 1-hour session with the new employee to review, discuss, and align. Both parties sign. Store in HRIS. This is the most important artifact this playbook produces.

#### Step 6.2 — First Real Work Assignment
**Executor:** human (New Employee)
**Owner:** Hiring Manager
**Deadline:** By end of Day 5
**Produces:** First task completed and reviewed; task entry in ledger

Assign one small, contained, real work task. Not busy work — something that produces actual value and gives the employee their first success in the role. Hiring manager reviews the output before Day 5 ends.

#### Step 6.3 — End-of-Week Check-in
**Executor:** human (HR Manager + Hiring Manager + New Employee)
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** Day 5
**Produces:** Check-in notes in HRIS; any open items flagged
**Boundary call:**
 type: human
 target: HR Manager
 validation: "Three-way check-in completed; findings documented in HRIS"
 max_retries: 0
**Pause after:** human-attestation

30-minute three-way meeting: what went well, what was confusing, what needs follow-up. HR Manager documents findings. Any open items become tasks.

---

### Group 7 — Benefits Enrollment
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Parallel:** yes (runs throughout the first 30 days, started by Group 3 Step 3.2)
**Depends on:** HR Documentation Complete (Group 3 Milestone — which opened the enrollment window)
**Milestone:** Benefits Enrollment Complete
**Milestone timeout:** Day 25 from start date — if not fired, send final reminder; Day 30 is hard deadline with no extension possible

#### Step 7.1 — Benefits Selection
**Executor:** human (New Employee) via HRIS
**Owner:** HR Manager
**Deadline:** Day 30 from start date (hard deadline — no extensions)
**Boundary call:**
 type: service
 target: hris-system
 validation: "Benefits elections confirmed and submitted in HRIS"
 max_retries: 0
 hard_deadline: Day 30 — if missed, employee must wait for next open enrollment or qualifying life event
**Pause after:** external-system

Invoke the [Benefits Enrollment] sub-playbook. Employee selects health, dental, vision, 401k, and any other available benefits. Agent sends reminders at Day 14 and Day 25 if enrollment is not complete.

## Verification

### Method

Multi-method: human sign-off for attestation gates, automated check for system records, peer review for 30/60/90 plan quality.

### Criteria

- All 6 required milestones appear as `event: milestone_fired` entries in `run.jsonl`
- HRIS shows: employment documents signed, compliance training completed, benefits enrolled
- `artifacts/30-60-90-plan.md` exists and is co-signed by hiring manager and employee
- No open `call_record` entries with `state: waiting` or `state: failed` in `run.jsonl`
- `playbook-runs.jsonl` entry for this run shows `status: complete`

### Cold-Boot Test Cases

Three independent agents verify this playbook is executable:

1. **Vague/exploratory:** "We have a new engineer starting Monday. Can you get their onboarding going?"
2. **Terse/direct:** "Run new-hire-onboarding for Jordan Lee, starting April 21, in-office, hiring manager is Sarah Chen."
3. **Contextual/referential:** "Jordan's onboarding is stuck — Group 4 can't start because Group 2 never fired its milestone. Can you diagnose and unblock?"

Expected for case 3: agent reads `run.jsonl`, identifies Physical Access Ready milestone as unfired, finds the open call record for Step 2.1, re-escalates to Facilities with context from `thread.md`.

### Escalation on Failure

- **Background check not cleared by Day -3:** Halt. Escalate to HR Director. Do not proceed.
- **Compliance training not complete by Day 14:** Flag to HR Director and Legal. Revoke production system access until complete.
- **Benefits enrollment window missed:** Notify employee and HR Director. Document in HRIS. Wait for next open enrollment.
- **30/60/90 not signed by Day 5:** Hiring manager escalation. If not resolved by Day 7, notify HR Director.

## Handoffs

- **HRIS System:** Employee record creation (Step 1.3), documentation storage (Steps 3.1, 3.2, 7.1), compliance tracking (Step 5.3), 30/60/90 storage (Step 6.1). Confirmation: system record exists with correct employee UID.
- **LMS:** Compliance training delivery and completion (Step 5.3). Confirmation: completion certificates present in HRIS.
- **Background Check Provider:** Clearance confirmation (Step 1.4). Confirmation: status 'cleared' logged.
