## Purpose

Captures the piece's parameters: angle (what specifically is the argument), audience (who is this for), surface (Substack primary, LinkedIn syndication, etc.), length (target word count), success criteria (what does "good" look like for this piece). Sets the frame the outline and draft will work against.

## Structure

Leaf step. No children.

## Nodes

No child nodes. Leaf WorkflowNode.

## Flow Rules

Completes when: the piece's parameters are captured as activation-root metadata or as a short define-piece document member of the activation root.

Advances to: outline (9a4b7d18).

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

The author or agent driving the run names the angle, the audience, the surface(s) the piece will publish to, the length target, and the success criteria. These can be captured as frontmatter fields on the activation-root project (lightest), or as a short bulleted document member (when the parameters warrant their own narrative). When committed, step-1 closes.

## Known Enforcement Gaps

None at v1.0.0. The parameters captured are convention rather than schema-enforced; future formalization (if patterns repeat) lives on the activation-root subtype's frontmatter schema.
