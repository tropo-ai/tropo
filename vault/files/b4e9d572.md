## Purpose

Govern the financial side of attending an industry conference as a vendor. Sponsorship contract; budget allocation; spend tracking through the activation; close-out reconciliation. Owned by `finance-coordinator-agent`.

## Structure

```
finance-workstream (workstream sub-pipeline)
├── Sponsorship contract negotiated + signed
├── Budget allocated across line items (booth / hospitality / swag / travel / etc.)
├── Spend tracked through activation
└── Close-out reconciliation (actual vs. budget; lessons for next activation)
```

## Nodes

Leaf workstream; tasks live as work items on the workstream's activation-root-project, generated by the workstream-owning agent against the master project plan.

## Flow Rules

Linear; tasks generated by `finance-coordinator-agent` against the cascade-generated project plan. No auto-task-spawn at this workstream (agent-owned per default).

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

When the master pipeline cascades, this workstream activates: `finance-coordinator-agent` reads the project plan's Finance deliverable + acceptance criteria, generates tasks (contract review; PO issuance; spend journal entries; reconciliation review), and works them through the activation lifecycle.

## Known Enforcement Gaps

None at v1.35.0 minimal demonstration shape. Richer per-workstream sub-stages defer to v1.35.5.
