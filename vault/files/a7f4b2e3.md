## Purpose

Plan and execute hospitality moments: sponsor reception, customer dinner, off-site touchpoints. Owned by `hospitality-coordinator-agent`.

## Structure

```
hospitality-workstream
├── Reception concept (theme; positioning; target guest profile)
├── Venue selection + booked
├── Menu + AV + entertainment
├── Guest list + invitations sent
├── On-night execution
└── Post-event follow-up (thank-you notes; lead capture)
```

## Nodes

Leaf workstream; tasks generated by `hospitality-coordinator-agent`.

## Flow Rules

Linear with cross-team dependencies (Staffing for executive-led greetings; Finance for venue contracting; Promotion for guest list curation).

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

`hospitality-coordinator-agent` generates tasks: venue shortlist + selection; menu tasting; invitation design + send; RSVP tracking; on-night production calls. Decisions surface at venue selection (with budget-impact rationale) and guest-list-cut moments.

## Known Enforcement Gaps

None at v1.35.0 minimal demonstration shape.
