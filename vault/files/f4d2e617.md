## Purpose

Authors and posts a syndication of the primary piece to a secondary surface (typically LinkedIn for essay-shaped content). The syndication post is a separate document that stands on its own, extracts the strongest standalone insight from the primary essay, and links back. NOT a copy-paste of the original.

## Structure

Leaf step. No children.

## Nodes

No child nodes. Leaf WorkflowNode.

## Flow Rules

Completes when: the syndication post is live on the secondary surface and the activation root's `linkedin_url:` (or surface-appropriate field) is populated.

Advances to: engage-comments (1b8c5a39).

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

At T+3-5 days after primary publication, the author writes the syndication post — a short standalone piece (250-500 words for LinkedIn) extracting the strongest insight from the primary essay and linking back. Authored as a separate `type: document` artifact, member of the activation root. Posted to the secondary surface. URL captured on activation root. The link to the primary surface is placed in the first comment per LinkedIn algorithm convention (external links in post body suppress reach).

## Known Enforcement Gaps

Per-surface syndication-post capsule (e.g., `pub-linkedin.capsule`) is deferred; uses generic `type: document` at v1.0.0. Auto-spawn of the syndication post at step-7 fire (parallel to dev-pipeline's release-plan → arch-spec auto-spawn) is deferred.
