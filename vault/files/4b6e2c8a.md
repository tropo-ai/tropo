*Placeholder person-entity that ships in every fresh install. Names the human (or AI principal) who owns this vault. The Concierge updates this entry on first interaction once the vault owner provides their name and pronouns.*

## What this is

Every Tropo-OS vault must name a **principal** — a person (or autonomous agent) whose authority grounds every vault-scope action. Without a principal, the D7 invariant cannot resolve: work-items must eventually chain ownership up to a person, and a vault-entity must have a person to point at.

This entry is the placeholder. It ships in fresh installs so that:
1. The vault-entity ([7c3a8e91](7c3a8e91.md)) has a valid `principal:` field on day one.
2. The vault-inbox ([2d5f9b04](2d5f9b04.md)) has a valid ownership chain on day one.
3. A new agent booting into the vault doesn't hit D7 violations before the concierge has run.

## What the Concierge does on first run

Per [STUDIO.md](../../STUDIO.md) `<FILL>` flow + concierge §1.5 (first-run identity capture):

1. Asks the user their name + preferred pronouns
2. Updates this entry's `title:`, `name:`, and adds optional fields like `pronouns:` to match
3. Logs the change in this entity's activity log
4. Confirms back to the user

**If the Concierge is bypassed**, this entry stays as "Vault Owner" — the vault still functions; the principal is just unnamed. No system-level breakage.

## Why this lives in the Vault as a placeholder

The alternative — leaving the vault-entity with a literal `<FILL: Your Name>` placeholder principal field — would fail entity.capsule Validation Check 1 (principal must resolve). A real (if generically-named) entity passes validation while still leaving room for the user to customize.

## Owner identity is the user's choice

If you (the vault owner) prefer to delete this entry and author your own person-entity from scratch, that's legal — just remember to:
1. Update [7c3a8e91](7c3a8e91.md) (vault-entity) `principal:` to point at your new entity
2. Update [2d5f9b04](2d5f9b04.md) (vault-inbox) ownership chain accordingly
3. Run `python3 .tropo/scripts/rebuild-vault.py --apply` (or your platform equivalent) to regenerate the vault index

The placeholder pattern is just a kindness — a working day-one default. Replace it whenever you're ready.
