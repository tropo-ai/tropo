## Purpose

The runtime-first-step of publish-pipeline. Transitions the source work-item from `accepted` to `active` by writing an acceptance record and updating its status field. Authors the activation-root project as the graph home for this run's outputs. Mirrors dev-pipeline's accept-work-item pattern.

## Structure

## Nodes

## Flow Rules

Completes when: (1) the source work-item's `accepted_by:` array has a new entry with `accepted_by_uid: <this-run-uid>` and `date_accepted: <today>`; (2) the work-item's `status:` is `active`; (3) the activation-root project is authored with `derived_from:` pointing at the source.

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

The executor reads the source work-item referenced by this run. It writes the acceptance record on the work-item, sets `status: active`, and authors the activation-root project (a `type: project` ledger entry named after the piece's working title, member of publish-pipeline). The source is added as the activation root's first member via `derived_from:`.
