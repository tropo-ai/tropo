Welcome to your vault. This project has five tasks — each one teaches you a piece of the system by having you actually use it.

You don't need to do them in order, but they're numbered so you have a starting point. By the time you finish task 2, you'll have created your own work and this vault will be yours.

Pick a task and start. Your agent can help with any of them.
