## What Was Done

Researched all residential solar permit requirements for Ridgewood County.

**Key findings:**
- Standard residential solar permit: Form RC-SOLAR-001, $285 fee, 15-business-day processing
- Utility interconnection application: separate submission to Ridgewood Municipal Utility, Form RMU-IC-04
- Inspection required: structural (pre-installation) + electrical (post-installation)
- **Non-obvious requirement:** Properties built before 1978 require a fire suppression addendum (Form RC-FS-ADD) — affects three of the ten pilot households

**Posted to coordination channel:** `PERMIT FINDING: Three households (IDs 03, 07, 09) require fire suppression addendum RC-FS-ADD. Vendor must confirm compatibility before final selection.`

## What This Enables

Vendor Coordination can now filter the shortlist for installers who are familiar with the RC-FS-ADD requirement. Customer Onboarding can flag the three affected households for additional paperwork.

The pilot does not proceed without this finding incorporated into vendor selection.
