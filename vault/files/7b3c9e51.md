## Purpose

The specify stage accepts the work-item, frames the piece (angle, audience, surface, length, success criteria), and produces an outline that the author stage will draft from. It is the first stage of publish-pipeline. Specify ends when the outline is committed and the piece's parameters are clear.

Does NOT govern: the source scan or brief that produced the work-item (that happened in the inbox pre-pipeline), or the actual draft authoring (that is author stage territory).

Linear, forward-only. Each step's `next_steps:` advances the executor when the prior step reaches `done`. step-2 (outline) is terminal in this stage; specify advances to author when its three steps complete.

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

The pipeline-run begins at specify stage. step-0 (accept-source) writes the acceptance record on the source work-item and authors the activation-root project. step-1 (define-piece) captures the piece's parameters. step-2 (outline) produces the outline document. When step-2 closes, specify advances to author.
