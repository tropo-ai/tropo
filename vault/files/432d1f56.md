## Purpose

Declares the Legal category within the web-target ship-artifact graph. Terms, policies, and legal notices ship through this category folder. Unlike the other three categories, Legal entries render inside React modal components — the content is short, load-bearing, and presented at a moment of user action (e.g., confirming download terms) rather than as browsable pages.

## Description

The Legal category surfaces legal content inside React modal components. The inaugural entry is the Tropo Download Terms (subtype: download-terms), rendered by the `DownloadTermsModal` component. Future legal surfaces (privacy policy, hosted-tier ToS) follow the same pattern with their own modal components.

Default cleanup rules per the manifest root (4a99638d §4): render only the `Modal-rendered content (load-bearing)` section of the body; strip section heading markers; strip Author's notes and all substrate-only commentary; do NOT rewrite UID-style links (legal content doesn't navigate users away — any links get stripped to plain text).

One child declared currently: the Download Terms entry (79e5fd33). Additional legal entries join this category as new legal surfaces are created.
