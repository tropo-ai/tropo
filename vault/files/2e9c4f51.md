## Purpose

Locks the draft for release. Incorporates any review feedback from step-4 if a reviewer was designated. Flips the draft document's lifecycle status (or equivalent capsule field) to publish-ready.

## Structure

Leaf step. No children. Terminal in author stage (advances to release stage at the stage level).

## Nodes

No child nodes. Leaf WorkflowNode.

## Flow Rules

Completes when: the draft document is locked (lifecycle status flipped to publish-ready or equivalent), with any review feedback incorporated.

Terminal in stage. Author stage advances to release when this step closes.

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

If a reviewer was designated at step-4 and provided feedback, the author incorporates the feedback. The draft's lifecycle status flips to indicate publish-ready (specific field depends on which capsule governs the draft — `type: document` at v1.0.0; future per-surface capsules may use different field names). When committed, step-5 closes; author stage advances to release stage.

## Known Enforcement Gaps

The "publish-ready" lifecycle field is per-capsule. At v1.0.0, drafts use generic `type: document` and convention captures the locked state. Future per-surface capsules formalize this.
