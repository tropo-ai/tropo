## What this is

Hello Tropo is the canonical worked example that ships in every fresh Tropo Studio. You're looking at the substrate a marketing team produced when they activated around attending an industry conference — the kind of multi-team coordination most marketing teams stitch together across Notion + Asana + Google Docs + Slack + agencies. Tropo holds it as one coherent governed unit of work.

**The work shape:** a master *industry-conference-activation* pipeline runs five stages (Decide → Plan → Coordinate → Execute → Wrap-up). At the Coordinate stage, the pipeline spawns six **workstreams** (sub-pipelines) — one per team that owns part of the work:

1. **Finance** — sponsorship contract, budget, spend tracking, close-out
2. **Booth** — design, vendor selection, build, ship, on-site setup
3. **Staffing** — who travels, sessions to attend, customer meetings scheduled
4. **Hospitality** — sponsor reception planning, venue, invites, menu
5. **Promotion** — pre/during/post-event social cadence, landing page, lead capture
6. **Content** — booth collateral, demo decks, datasheets, post-event nurture

Each workstream-owning agent generates their own tasks against the project plan. The Promotion workstream additionally demonstrates **auto-task-spawning** via cascade — a teach-by-example for users who want to see how Tropo can pattern-match a repeatable cadence (pre-event tease, day-of-teaser, post-event recap) onto a date-keyed task list.

## What you'll see

- **The master pipeline-run** — the activation that fired this whole campaign
- **The six workstream activations** — each with its own activation-root-project + tasks
- **The project plan** — generated by the cascade; coordinates across workstreams
- **The work items** — tasks at terminal states across each workstream
- **The project board** — state-at-a-glance dashboard rendered to `00-tropo-nav/`
- **The wrap-up retro** — what worked, what didn't, lessons for the next activation

The substrate is real. The work shape is what marketing teams actually do. Swap the conference for your own activation; swap the workstream owners for your own team's roles; the pattern transposes cleanly.

## How to navigate

- Open the project board (rendered at `00-tropo-nav/00-tropo-active/hello-tropo/board.md`) for a state-at-a-glance view.
- Open the master pipeline (cited in the board's pipeline-stage-progression section) to see the workflow definition.
- Drill into any workstream activation to see its work items.
- Tropo (the permanent first-encounter agent) can walk you through any of this and answer questions about how a piece works.
