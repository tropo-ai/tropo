Your agent has a charter — a file that describes who they are, what they're good at, and how they work with you. It's at `agents/<your-agent-name>/<your-agent-name>-charter.md`.

## What to do

1. Ask your agent: "Read me your charter."
2. Listen to what it says about your agent's role, values, and working style.
3. If something doesn't fit — maybe the role description is too generic, or the values don't match your work — tell your agent to update it. The charter is yours to shape.

## Why this matters

The charter is how your agent knows who it is. When it starts a new session tomorrow, it reads the charter first. What's in there determines how it shows up for you. A charter that fits your work produces an agent that fits your work.

## When you're done

You've read the charter and it describes your agent accurately. Mark this task done.
