## Purpose

After both trigger steps (4.5 doc + 4.6 test) fire, the dev-cycle's owner posts a direct surface to each triggered pipeline's owner's bilateral channel naming the activation UID, spec UID, cycle context, and the "awaiting your processing" framing.

This step closes the structural gap that v1.52 first production cycle exposed: trigger-step authors the activation entries + writes the spec UIDs into dev-spec frontmatter, but neither doc-pipeline owner nor test-pipeline owner gets any signal their work is active until they explicitly check. The board-surfacing piece (sa.board-agent catches the activation on owner's next boot) is the persistent visibility lane; this step is the immediate channel-inbox signal.

Does NOT govern: the triggered pipelines' own work (that's the doc-pipeline + test-pipeline definitions' job). Does NOT cover: pipeline-runs where the engine is invoked entirely automated (no agent executor available to post channels); future v1.53+ refinement may engine-automate the channel posts.

## Flow Rules

**Activates when:** both step 4.5 (0cf86ea5 trigger-doc) AND step 4.6 (4f64ec3c trigger-test) reach status:done — multi-input convergence on both triggers. Engine surfaces this step as eligible-next once both predecessors are verified.

**Completes when:** both exit_criteria pass — channel posts landed at doc-pipeline owner's bilateral channel + test-pipeline owner's bilateral channel.

**Advances to:** step 5 produce-release-folder (8654900a) — single-input handoff. Step 5's `depends_on_steps:` amended at v1.52 from [0cf86ea5, 4f64ec3c] to [37996741] (this step becomes the single gate for produce-release-folder; the chain now goes triggers → notify → produce-release-folder instead of triggers → produce-release-folder).

**Failure modes:** if channel posts fail to land (write permission error, channel file missing, etc.), step stays in `eligible` state; executor addresses + retries. Not a ship-blocker by construction — the channel post is informational; if owners are already aware through other channels (Mike pinged them, board boot picked up the activation), the step still gates produce-release-folder until the post is on-record per substrate-honesty discipline.

## Engine Contract — exit criteria

Two exit_criteria evaluated via grep against channel files:

- `channel post landed at doc-pipeline owner's bilateral channel` — DSL resolves doc-pipeline def [5a4337ff] owner field; looks for the activation UID (e.g., 461b3896) cited in the most recent post at channels/argus-<owner>.md
- `channel post landed at test-pipeline owner's bilateral channel` — same shape on test-pipeline def [da3f50dc] owner field; channels/argus-<owner>.md

If both pass: `verify-step 37996741` returns `pass`; step marks complete.

If either fails: step stays `eligible`; executor reposts.

## Cold-Boot Walk-Through

A dev-pipeline-run enters step 4.7 with:

- Steps 4.5 (0cf86ea5) + 4.6 (4f64ec3c) both at status:done
- doc-pipeline activation exists at vault/files/<doc-activation-uid>.md with status:active + triggered_spec_uid:<doc-spec-uid>
- test-pipeline activation exists at vault/files/<test-activation-uid>.md with status:active + triggered_spec_uid:<test-spec-uid>

Argus (step_owner_role) executes:

1. **Resolve doc-pipeline owner:** read doc-pipeline def [5a4337ff] frontmatter; extract `owner:` field (e.g., `orpheus`).
2. **Resolve test-pipeline owner:** read test-pipeline def [da3f50dc] frontmatter; extract `owner:` field (e.g., `vela`).
3. **Post to doc-pipeline owner's bilateral channel** at `channels/argus-<owner>.md`. Format: `[<date>] Argus A<N> → <Owner> | Doc-pipeline activation triggered — awaiting your processing | 🚦` with one-paragraph body naming: triggered activation UID, doc-spec UID, cycle context (cycle UID + brief title), what the activation expects (the agent runs the activation through its stages to status:done), and the dev-pipeline cycle ship gate dependency.
4. **Post to test-pipeline owner's bilateral channel** at `channels/argus-<owner>.md`. Same format with test-pipeline substitutions.
5. **Fire step-complete:** `pipeline-runtime.py step-complete 37996741 --artifact-links <doc-channel-post-uri> <test-channel-post-uri>` (or grep-verifiable paths to the channel posts).
6. **Fire verify-step:** `pipeline-runtime.py verify-step 37996741` — engine evaluates the two exit_criteria via grep; returns pass.

Step 4.7 marks complete. Engine surfaces step 5 (8654900a produce-release-folder) as eligible.
